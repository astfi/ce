IF YOU ARE THINKING OF CONSOLIDATING EXISTING BORROWING YOU SHOULD BE AWARE THAT YOU MAY BE EXTENDING THE TERM OF THE DEBT AND INCREASING THE TOTAL AMOUNT YOU REPAY.

We will remind you about this if we introduce you to Asset Finance or Allied and will only introduce you to Asset Finance or Allied where it is in your best interests.
